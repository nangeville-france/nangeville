Geographic setting

Nangeville lies in the north of the Loiret department, in the Centre-Val de Loire region. Since 1 January 2016, the village has been part of the new commune of Le Malesherbois, which brings together several former communes of the area. Its postcode is 45330.

Reading caveat — small sample. With a population of 112 inhabitants, relative indicators (density, indices, percentage shares) are very sensitive to small absolute variations: the arrival or departure of a few families can noticeably change these indicators from one year to the next. These figures should therefore be read as orders of magnitude, not as precise measurements. The density shown is calculated (population ÷ area) for a single reference year (2022).
And what about a comparison with the department or France? A density comparison is only rigorous if all the values come from the same reference year and from a homogeneous source. Lacking a consistent, recent dataset bringing together the commune, the Loiret, the region and France for an identical year, this comparison is deliberately not presented here, rather than juxtaposing figures from different years.

The territory

Area: 8.59 km²

The commune extends over 8.59 km² of open countryside. The landscape is that, typical of the Gâtinais, of large cereal-growing lands: gently rolling arable plains, dotted with farms, copses and country lanes. The altitude varies gently, from 109 to 142 metres, with no marked relief.

Seen from above, the territory forms a mosaic characteristic of the Gâtinais: vast cultivated plots that cover most of the plateau, a few woods and copses, meadows along the low-lying points, and a village core clustered around its church, linked to the rest of the commune by a network of roads and country lanes. It is this same logic of dividing land by use that the Napoleonic cadastre described in the 19th century.

This agricultural vocation is nothing new: it continues a centuries-old tradition of tilling and stock-rearing that has shaped the village and its inhabitants for generations.

Community life

In rural communes like Nangeville, local life often revolves around associations that bring residents together and maintain the social fabric of the village.

Local hunting association. Like many rural communes of the Loiret, Nangeville most likely has an approved local hunting association (ACCA). These associations, overseen by the Fédération départementale des chasseurs du Loiret (Loiret Departmental Hunters' Federation), manage local game populations — roe deer, hares, pheasants — in keeping with regional hunting plans and the area's farming practices.

Local life today

Since 2016, Nangeville has been a delegated commune within Le Malesherbois. The village keeps its name, its coat of arms and its identity, but most local services — town hall, school, administrative services — are now pooled at the scale of Le Malesherbois.

In practice: for your administrative formalities, your schooling or most public services, the new commune of Le Malesherbois is the point of reference. Nangeville remains one of its villages, attached to its history and its rural setting.
