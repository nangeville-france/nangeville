Municipality · March 2026
New municipal team elected

Following the municipal elections of March 2026, the list "Unis pour Le Malesherbois", led by Hervé Gaurat, won the vote. The new municipal council was installed at its inaugural meeting in late March 2026, at which Hervé Gaurat was elected mayor of Le Malesherbois.

Source: ville-lemalesherbois.fr, March 2026
Municipal council · Ongoing
Municipal council minutes

The Le Malesherbois municipal council meets publicly at 7 p.m. at the Centre Culturel Le Grand Écrin in Malesherbes. Meeting agendas and minutes are published regularly on the official site.
